================================================================================
THE WINNING CORE FEATURE & USP: THE CAREER READINESS INDEX (CRI)
================================================================================
Unlike standard resume editors that guess ATS keywords, our "Employer Perspective 
Engine" evaluates the student's *entire profile* dynamically:
1. Technical quiz scores from the Training Center.
2. STAR-method scores from the voice-enabled AI Mock Interview simulator.
3. Resume length, density, active verb usage, and graduation milestones.

THE OUTPUT: A weighted, diagnostic Career Readiness Index (0-100) scoring 6 core 
dimensions:
- Technical Skills
- Project Experience
- Industry Exposure
- Communication Skills
- Problem Solving Ability
- Resume Strength

The student immediately knows their rating (e.g. "Startup Ready" vs. "Ready for 
Big Tech") and receives an interactive checklist of the Top 10 Recruiter Risk 
Factors they need to check off to become hireable.

   Interview Selection %) and check off action plans.
